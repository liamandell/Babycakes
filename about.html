About Us

At Babycakes Bakery, we're more than just a bake shop — we're a community staple, a family-run business, and a place where every treat is made with heart.

Founded in 2010 in the heart of Austin, Babycakes started as a weekend pop-up by two sisters who shared a passion for pastries and people. Over the years, we’ve grown into a cozy brick-and-mortar shop serving hand-crafted desserts, locally sourced ingredients, and the same love we started with.

Whether you're grabbing your morning croissant, ordering a cake for your big day, or just stopping by for a warm cookie and a chat — we’re happy to have you.
